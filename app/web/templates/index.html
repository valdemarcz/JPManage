Adding a car to JPManage

A step-by-step guide how to add your car to this system.

Note: in order to add your car to the system you have to login or sign up.

Not logged-in users can only search for cars which are currently up for sale.

Once you have logged in, indicate the make of your car.
*Certain makes are absent in our system. However, we are working on adding them to the system.

How to add an offer:

  • Sign up / login
  • Press "Add new car" from the header.
  • If you want to find out the price of your car, fill in all data before "Features" except VIN.
  • After filling in all data press "Proposed price" to learn the 2 prices generated by our AI algorithms.
  • If you are willing to propose your car for us, choose one of the generated prices or propose your own.
  • You may also add photos, additional features or descriptions of your car.
  • Pressing "Send offer" will add your car to our database.
  • Every logged-in user can see his/her cars by pressing "My cars" on header.
  • Note: a notification will appear if you haven't added a car.
  • Once your proposition is analysed by the administrator, it shall be either accepted or rejected.
  • If your offer is accepted, you may contact us in order to discuss the details.

Searching for cars in JPManage

a step-by-step guide how to search for cars in the system.

You may search cars in our system without having to login.

  • Press "Search" in the header.
  • Pressing "Search" without choosing any option will show you all current offers.
  • Indicate the make of the car before choosing its model. The list of car models depends on a particular car make.
  • Once you have indicated all the requirements, a list of offers will appear.
  • Note: a notification will appear if no car fulfills your requirements.
  • To buy a car from our list you will need to contact us by contact form or by calling us.
  • Note: all prices are confirmed by an expert. Nonetheless, negotiation in regards to the price is possible.
